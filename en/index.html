Frequently Asked Questions

How does Retuned measure my stress?

Retuned uses your phone's camera to detect micro-variations in your skin color caused by your heartbeat (a technique called PPG). From this data, we calculate your heart rate variability — a scientific indicator of your nervous system's state.

Does it actually work?

Audio biofeedback technology has been used in clinical settings for decades. Retuned makes it accessible and automatic: the sound adapts to your body in real-time, without you needing to focus or follow instructions.

What's different from other apps?

Most apps ask you to follow instructions or concentrate. Retuned asks nothing of you: put on your headphones, and your body does the work. No guided voice, no technique to learn. Just sound that adapts to you.

How long is a session?

A typical session lasts 10 to 15 minutes. You can see the effects on your heart rate variability in real-time, and the app suggests when your nervous system has returned to a calm state.
